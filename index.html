How it works

A simple process from messy files to usable results.

Let us take care of the work for you. Send the file, explain the outcome you need, and Momentum Data will help turn the work into a clear deliverable.

1

Share the file or data issue

Send a sample file, document, export, or description of the manual process you want improved.

2

Confirm the scope

We define the required fields, output format, cleanup rules, timeline, and final deliverable.

3

Extract, clean, and validate

Your data is organized, checked for consistency, and prepared for use in reports or systems.

4

Deliver usable output

You receive a clean spreadsheet, report, dashboard, workflow, or data-ready template.
