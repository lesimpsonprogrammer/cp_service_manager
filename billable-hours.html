Learning Logic

How CPSM improves future estimates

1. Capture actualsEvery saved timecard stores actual hours by task, client, project category, and PM/user.
2. Calculate averagesCPSM recalculates average completion time after completed entries are saved.
3. Suggest estimateFuture entries use historical averages to suggest estimated hours before the PM submits.
4. Compare varianceEstimated WTD, Actual WTD, and variance show whether project time is pacing correctly.
